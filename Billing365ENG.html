Knowing the Porcess

Global Solution

Our vision for the billing ecosystem focuses on the comprehensive mastery of the financial lifecycle. We understand this process not merely as the issuance of documents, but as a seamless orchestration spanning from provisions (Unbilled) management to invoices. We are structuring this model to serve as a natural extension of our current infrastructure, ensuring that operational control and regional regulatory compliance remain the core pillars supporting every transaction

Regulatory Compliance Alignment

The process of converting a provision into an invoice must comply with specific tax requirements (such as those of the DIAN in Colombia or the SUNAT in Peru). This highlights that the system automatically understands the validation, tax, and electronic invoicing rules of each region

End-to-End Traceability

It's not just about creating a provision; it's about knowing exactly where it comes from and which invoice it ends up on. Ensuring that every accounting provision has a clear path to becoming a legally binding invoice prevents revenue leakage and ensures that no outstanding provisions remain in the staging tables
